Being humorous is a skill. But not letting your humour affect other people is an art. Very often when there are a bunch of friends chit-chatting, we try to prove ourselves humorous by pulling others down, which is ok at times.
But trying to get better at your skills, you into the mode of pulling ppl down always! We assume that it lightens up the environment. It does. But have we thought about the person being mocked at? Either they try to defend themselves with a counter statement or their confidence level drops a bit and they get demotivated to not try something new.
How often have you appreciated someone for what they do or for their looks or for their clothes?
